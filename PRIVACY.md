# Privacy Policy — Scaffold

**Effective Date:** March 25, 2026

---

## Overview

Scaffold is a free, open-source Claude Code plugin developed by Alex Xenakis. It runs entirely on your local machine. This privacy policy exists for transparency: **Scaffold does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data.**

---

## What Data Scaffold Collects

**None.**

Scaffold has no servers, no backend, no database, no API endpoints, no telemetry, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no tracking of any kind. There is no user account system, no registration, and no authentication.

---

## Local Storage

Scaffold creates and manages files on your local filesystem only. These include memory files, session logs, configuration files, and Obsidian vault entries. All of this data:

- Resides exclusively on your machine
- Is never transmitted anywhere by Scaffold
- Is fully under your control
- Can be viewed, modified, or deleted at any time using your file explorer or any text editor

Scaffold does not access, read, or modify files outside its designated working directories unless you explicitly instruct it to.

---

## Third-Party Services

Scaffold is a plugin for **Claude Code**, a product developed and operated by **Anthropic**. Scaffold itself makes no external network requests. However, because Scaffold operates within the Claude Code environment, your interactions are processed by Anthropic's systems according to their own terms and policies.

For details on how Anthropic handles your data, refer to:

- [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
- [Anthropic Terms of Service](https://www.anthropic.com/terms)

Scaffold has no control over, affiliation with, or responsibility for Anthropic's data practices.
